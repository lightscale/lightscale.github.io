Technology

We use chromatography for all required tests, a technique where liquid or gas separates different cannabinoids, pesticides, and residual solvents so that each can be identified and measured precisely.

GC-MS

Our gas chromatography mass spectrometer detects residual solvents used in cannabis extraction to ensure any remaining amounts are below legally acceptable levels.

LC-MS

We use our liquid chromatography mass spectrometer to detect trace levels of potentially harmful pesticides, some down to the parts per billion.

HPLC

High performance liquid chromatography is used to measure the potency of cannabinoids like THC and CBD in cannabis flower and cannabis-derived products.
